#summary Consumer Goods

= Introduction =

Consumer Goods are TradeGoods bought and used by colonists. They are split into four categories which match a different need of the population.

= Food =

  * Luxury Food
  * Ready Meals
  * Rations

= Drink =

  * Ambrosia
  * Soda
  * Water

= Intoxicant =

  * Narcotics
  * Stimulants
  * Alcohol

= Clothes =

  * Luxury Clothes
  * Regular Clothes
  * Bargain Clothes
